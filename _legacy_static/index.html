Our Mission

We Stand With Victims. We Fight for Justice.

AVASC was founded by people who experienced the trauma of cyber-scams firsthand. We know the shame, the loss, and the frustration of feeling powerless. Our mission is to change that — through community, education, and action.

Victim Advocacy
We amplify victim voices and advocate for stronger cybercrime enforcement and legislation.
Community Support
Connect with other survivors, share experiences, and find peer support in a safe, judgment-free space.
Public Education
We educate communities to recognize, avoid, and report cyber-scams before they cause harm.

The Process

Four Steps to Getting Help

We've made it as simple as possible to get the support and resources you need, when you need them most.

Reach Out
Contact us through our secure online form, hotline, or email. All communications are completely confidential.
Case Review
A trained volunteer reviews your situation and connects you with the right resources and support team.
Action Plan
We create a personalized roadmap covering reporting, recovery steps, and emotional support resources.
Ongoing Support
Recovery takes time. We stay with you throughout the journey with check-ins, community access, and follow-up resources.

About AVASC

Founded by Victims. Built on Truth.

AVASC was established to give a voice and a support system to the millions of Americans harmed by cyber-scams each year. We are a community — not a corporation. Every dollar donated, every hour volunteered, goes directly toward supporting victims and preventing future harm.


Our founder's personal experience with a devastating online scam became the catalyst for building an organization that treats victims with dignity, provides real actionable support, and fights for systemic change in how cybercrime is prosecuted and prevented.
